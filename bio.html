About Me

Overview

I'm David Jin, a final year undergraduate computer science student at Northwestern University. I am an ambitious individual and critical and analytical thinker. To me, nothing feels better than decomposing and logically solving complex problems, and I am always reflecting on experiences to learn and identify ways to improve. My primary interests lie in networking and systems.

I was born in Toronto, Canada, where I lived for 12 years before moving to Chicago, USA. As a result, I have a great affinity for Canada and the UK. In my free time, I enjoy listening to Oasis and other British rock, playing guitar, and watching soccer and Formula 1.
